Top Attractions

Varkala Beach

Varkala Beach, located in the southern Indian state of Kerala, is a stunning coastal paradise known for its dramatic cliffs, golden sands, and serene Arabian Sea views. Unlike other crowded beaches, Varkala offers a peaceful retreat, attracting travelers seeking relaxation and natural beauty. The beach is famous for its red laterite cliffs that rise majestically alongside the shoreline, offering breathtaking sunset vistas. Small cafes and shops line the cliff edge, serving fresh seafood, tropical drinks, and handmade crafts. Varkala is also spiritually significant, home to the ancient Janardanaswamy Temple, where pilgrims come to perform rituals. The natural spring near the beach is believed to have healing properties, adding to the area's mystical charm. Yoga centers and Ayurvedic spas are scattered throughout the town, making it a popular wellness destination. Whether you're looking for soulful solitude, scenic beauty, or cultural depth, Varkala Beach is a place that lingers in the heart long after you leave.

Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple

Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple, located in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala, is one of the most sacred and mysterious temples in India. Dedicated to Lord Vishnu, the deity is depicted in a reclining posture on the serpent Anantha, an image both majestic and deeply symbolic. The temple's architecture blends Dravidian and Kerala styles, with intricate carvings, towering gopurams, and a sanctum steeped in devotion. What truly elevated the temple to global fame was the discovery of hidden vaults containing immense treasures—gold ornaments, gems, and antique artifacts—making it one of the richest temples in the world. Only Hindus are allowed entry, and strict traditional dress codes are observed, preserving its sanctity. Beyond its wealth, the temple is a symbol of deep spiritual heritage, royal patronage, and timeless devotion. The annual Alpashy and Painkuni festivals attract thousands of devotees, adding vibrant energy to this serene, holy site. Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple is truly a divine marvel of India.
